The compact window
A 360 × ~280px always-on-top window that stays visible throughout the interview. Larger than the previous pill design and carries more information: current status headline, a finding preview when flags exist, a scan-history timeline (showing the trend of recent scans so both parties can see whether the machine stayed clean), countdown to the next periodic scan, and primary action buttons. When periodic scanning is on, a subtle progress bar fills along the bottom as the 5-minute interval elapses. When periodic is off, the history shows only the most recent scan and the countdown is replaced with "Periodic scanning off." Does not exclude itself from screen capture — both the candidate and the interviewer see it during the screen-share. The drag handle at the top lets the candidate tuck it into any corner.
