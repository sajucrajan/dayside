The full window
1040 × 640px, capped height. The Ready state is horizontal — hero on the left, options on the right. The Results state leads with a hero KPI row (Critical / Warnings / Clean), then splits into a 220px sidebar and a content pane. The sidebar is the tab selector — click Processes, Windows, Browser tabs, Audio & video, Monitors, Remote access, or System state, and only that category's list is shown in the content pane. Processes is active by default so the critical flag is the first thing visible. The content pane scrolls internally if the list is long; the window itself stays at a fixed height. Active sidebar item shows a severity-colored accent bar on its left edge.
